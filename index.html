About Me

I’m an aspiring project manager and developer with a passion for building thoughtful, impactful technology. My journey into computer science began with a hands-on internship where I worked on a platform supporting Black- and Brown-owned businesses, an experience that shaped both my technical skills and my perspective on meaningful design. Since then, I’ve expanded my skill set across front-end development and design, working with tools like React, HTML, CSS, and Figma. I enjoy bridging creativity with functionality—turning ideas into intuitive, user-centered experiences. As I continue growing in tech, I’m especially interested in the intersection of AI and business, and I’m driven to contribute to projects that are both innovative and ethically grounded.
